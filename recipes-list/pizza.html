Pizza

It's easy to make this thin crust pizza dough recipe with a few ingredients you may likely already have on hand. Here's what you'll need:

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Yeast: This thin crust pizza dough starts with one teaspoon of active dry yeast.
  2. Sugar and water: Activate the yeast in white sugar and lukewarm water.
  3. Flour: This crispy thin crust calls for all-purpose flour instead of bread flour
  4. Salt: Salt tightens the gluten structure, which strengthens the dough and makes it more elastic.
